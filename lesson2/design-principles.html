Visual Hierarchy

Cartier

Website

Cartier uses the principle of Visual Hierarchy by using a larger and bolder font for the title and a thinner and lighter font at the bottom of the page, which guide the user through the information.

PARC: Repetition

Mont Tremblant

Website

Mont Tremblant's website uses the principle of Repetition to promote the different activities by maintaining the same size, fonts, and distribution of the section.

White Space and Clean Design

Disneyland Resort

Website

This website uses the principle of White Space and Clean Design by keeping fewer elements with a prominent white space that makes the website easy to read and get all the information.
