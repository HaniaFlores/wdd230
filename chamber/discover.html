Pictures of Nuevo Leon

Fundidora Park

Fundidora Park (Parque Fundidora in Spanish) is an urban park located in the Mexican city of Monterrey, built in what once were the grounds of the Monterrey Foundry, the first steel and iron foundry in Latin America, and, for many years, the most important one in the region.
LEGO Factory

The world's largest LEGO Group factory. The LEGO Group officially opened a 33,850 m² expansion at its factory in Monterrey, part of a $US500 million investment in the site which will increase production capacity to meet growing, long-term demand in the Americas region and globally.

The Macroplaza

The Macroplaza or La Gran Plaza is a town square or plaza located in the heart of the city of Monterrey, Mexico. The Macroplaza is the fifth-largest plaza in the world and the largest Plaza in Mexico. It has an extension of 400,000 square metres consisting of various monuments, smaller plazas and gardens.
Metropolitan Center

Located right across the street of Fashin Drive, a nice and secure area in San Pedro municipality. This is not a shopping center but a restaurant area. It combines the best of the best cuisines such as steaks houses, seafood, regional, fusion, Italian, etc.

PepsiCo is a global food and beverage leader with a product portfolio including 22 brands that generate more than $1 billion each in annual retail sales.
