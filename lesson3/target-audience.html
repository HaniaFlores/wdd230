Target Audience

There will be two big groups of audience. The first is Entrepreneurs, start-ups, and new & potential businesses and the second group is Pro-business members that care about advocacy and the local business environment.

Personas

Persona 1: An owner of a start-up around 25-30 years old who is looking to increase their profits and develop their company after graduating from college. Without a lot of experience, thatâ€™s why they are looking for the opportunity to talk with other entrepreneurs and share knowledge. This persona is very familiarized with new technologies so it can access our website through a computer or smartphone.

Persona 2: A pro-business member around 45-50 years old who wants to have a major participation in the community and is looking for more colleagues to improve the economic scenario of the area. Cares about the business environment and wants to invest in some new ideas and start new projects in the community. They have a lot of market experience but most of them are not very comfortable with electronic devices, the best way for them to get access to our website is through a computer.

Scenarios

  • Other business owners told me about United, what is the price of their memberships?
  • I want to meet other entrepreneurs and make deals with them.
  • I want to bring my company to this area, is this the correct place?
  • What are the benefits of joining the chamber?
  • Is this program for me?
