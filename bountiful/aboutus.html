Our History

Bountiful Foods was founded with the vision to provide the community with fresh, organic and locally grown produce. The small business is based in Carlsbad, California, but has expanded its reach through its food trucks that cater to customers along the coastal areas from Los Angeles to San Diego.

The company sources its fruits and produce directly from local farmers, ensuring that customers get the freshest and most nutritious options available. Additionally, we use some national and international suppliers to offer a wider variety of fruits and produce.

The company is committed to promoting healthy eating habits and offers a range of organic, made-to-order fruit drinks and fruit bags. Bountiful Foods aims to provide convenience for customers who are looking for fresh, healthy food options, whether they are locals or tourists visiting the coastal areas.
